# Threats to Validity

Potential limitations in ANR evaluation include:

## Model bias

Different models may respond differently to the same repository structure, which can bias results.

## Repository size

Small repositories may overstate benefits while large monorepos may introduce complexity not captured in pilot studies.

## Domain differences

Outcomes can vary by domain (web, backend, data, infra), reducing direct comparability.

## Team maturity

Teams with stronger engineering hygiene may get better ANR results regardless of the architecture itself.

These factors should be explicitly documented in study design and result interpretation.
